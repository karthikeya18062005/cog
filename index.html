Top 10 SQL Query Hotlist

  1. Find the Nth highest salary from an `employees` table. (Medium) - Asked ~18 times (2022-2024)
  2. Select all employees who do not belong to a specific department. (Easy) - Asked ~15 times (2022-2024)
